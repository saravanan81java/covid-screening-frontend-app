Occupational Health and Safety Tips

For more occupational health and safety related information, please visit the Departmental information on iService (only available on the internal network) or the COVID-19 page on Canada.ca.

Remember when in the workplace to follow all protocols and instructions:

  • Respect occupancy limits.
  • Wear an approved mask.
  • Practice physical distancing.
  • Follow directional signage and floor markings.
  • Sneeze and cough into your elbow.
  • Use proper hand hygiene; wash your hands or use hand sanitizer.
